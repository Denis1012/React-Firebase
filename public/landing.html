About Us

What is X-Purt?

X-Purt is a mobile and web platform designed to connect high schoolers to the resources and counselors they need to navigate the college applications process. Started by Stanford students passionate about education, and built with input from dozens of Professional Counselors, X-Purt is the platform that we wish that we had in high school.

Access to Valuable College Application Resources

At X-Purt our primary concern is ensuring that students are best prepared for the applications process, so we provide free resources to best helo students!

Appointments

Book appointments with college students and professional consultants on the platform. You can book from 30 minutes to an hour, and can book as many sessions as you like!

Ask Questions on the Forum

Ask other students, as well as college students and college counsultants any questions that you can think of! We moderate the forum so everything is appropriate and relevant!

Security

Every college student and professional consultant undergoes a background check before being allowed to use the platform, so you can be assured of safety. We also have a reporting process if there are any issues!
